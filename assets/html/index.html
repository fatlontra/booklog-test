About Book Log

Welcome to Book Log! This app helps you track, rate, and organize your books effortlessly. Whether you’re into Sci-Fi, Fantasy, or Horror, add your favorites, edit details, or remove them anytime. Your reading journey stays organized and accessible—perfect for book lovers!

Key Features

  • Add and manage books seamlessly.
  • Secure biometric login (coming soon).
  • Edit or delete books easily.
  • AI chatbot assistance (coming soon).
